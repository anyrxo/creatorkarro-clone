## Lesson 1: The Pre-Launch (Building Hype)

The mistake most creators make: "Surprise! I made a thing. Buy it."
*Result*: Crickets. People need time to get excited. They need to feel like they are part of the journey.

**The "Open Loop" Strategy (14 Days Out)**:
Start talking about the *problem* and the *process*, not the product.

- **Post 1 (The Problem)**: "I've been struggling with [Problem] for years. It's so frustrating because [Pain Points]. Has anyone else felt this?"
    - *Goal*: Agitate the pain. Get "Yes" comments.
- **Post 2 (The Epiphany)**: "I think I cracked the code. I tried [New Method] and look at these results... [Screenshot]."
    - *Goal*: Build curiosity. "How did you do that?"
- **Post 3 (The Tease)**: "A lot of you asked how I did this. I'm putting together a guide. Who would be interested?" (Poll).
    - *Goal*: Validate demand.
- **Post 4 (The Behind-the-Scenes)**: Share a photo of your messy desk or you writing the ebook. "Coming soon... putting the finishing touches on the [Product Name]."
    - *Goal*: Show effort.

## Lesson 2: The Waitlist Strategy

Don't launch to the public first. Launch to the VIPs.

**Why a Waitlist?**
1.  **Psychology**: "Waitlist" implies exclusivity and demand.
2.  **Algorithm**: Social media reach is unreliable. Email is 100% delivery.
3.  **Momentum**: You want a flood of sales in the first hour to prove social proof.

**The Waitlist Landing Page**:
- **Headline**: "The Ultimate Guide to [Result]."
- **CTA**: "Join the waitlist for early access + a 50% discount code."
- **Link**: Put this in your bio 7 days before launch.

**The "Early Bird" Email**:
- Send the link to the waitlist 24 hours before public launch.
- **Subject**: "It's live (for you)."
- **Body**: "Because you're on the waitlist, you get first dibs. Here is your 50% off code. It expires in 48 hours."

## Lesson 3: The 5-Day Launch Sequence

Once you go public, follow this schedule. Do not skip a day.

**Day 1: The Announcement (Cart Open)**
- **Theme**: "It's Finally Here!"
- **Action**: Post the cover, the benefits, and the link.
- **Urgency**: "Launch price ends in 5 days."
- **Email**: "The [Product] is LIVE. Here's what's inside..."

**Day 2: The Social Proof**
- **Theme**: "Look who's already winning."
- **Action**: Share screenshots of early buyers, DMs saying "Just bought!", or beta tester testimonials.
- **Psychology**: FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out). "Everyone else is buying it."

**Day 3: The FAQ (Objection Handling)**
- **Theme**: "Questions?"
- **Action**: "I've been getting some questions..." (Answer the top 3 objections: Is this for beginners? Do I need software? etc.)
- **Email**: "Q&A: Is this right for you?"

**Day 4: The "What You Get" (Logic)**
- **Theme**: The Stack.
- **Action**: Show a video scrolling through the product. "Here is exactly what's inside."
- **Email**: "A look inside the course..."

**Day 5: The Close (Cart Closing)**
- **Theme**: "Last Chance."
- **Action**:
    - Morning: "12 hours left to get the discount."
    - Evening: "Doors closing in 2 hours."
- **Email 1 (AM)**: "Last day to save $50."
- **Email 2 (PM)**: "Goodbye [Product] discount."
- *Note*: **50% of your sales will happen on the last day.** Do not be afraid to post multiple times.

## Lesson 4: Organic Traffic Sources

Where do you find buyers without ads?

**1. Instagram Stories (The Closer)**
- Best for selling to existing followers.
- Use link stickers.
- Post 5-10 times a day during launch.
- Use Countdowns, Polls, and Q&A stickers to boost engagement.

**2. TikTok/Reels (The Reach)**
- Best for finding *new* people.
- Create a "Pinned Video" explaining the offer.
- Go Live to answer questions.
- **Hook**: "Stop doing [Old Way]. Here is the [New Way]."

**3. Twitter/Threads (The Builder in Public)**
- "I just launched X. Here is a thread on why I built it."
- Pin the tweet.
- Reply to people talking about the problem with a link to your solution.

**4. Cold DMs (The Hustle)**
- *Only for high-ticket ($200+)*.
- Don't spam.
- **Script**: "Hey [Name], I saw you were asking about [Topic] in that comment section. I actually just made a guide on exactly that. Happy to send over the link if you want to check it out? No pressure."

## Lesson 5: The "Launch Party" Livestream

Go live on Instagram/TikTok on Day 1 or Day 5.

**The Format**:
1.  **Teach**: Give 15 minutes of free value related to the topic.
2.  **Pitch**: "If you want to go deeper, I cover this in my new course..."
3.  **Q&A**: Answer questions live.
4.  **Bonus**: "If you buy while I'm live, DM me the screenshot and I'll send you an extra bonus."
